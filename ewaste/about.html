Our Mission

The E-Waste Management Project at Nazareth College of Arts and Science promotes responsible disposal, recycling, and reuse of electronic waste. With the ever-increasing consumption of electronics, managing this waste sustainably is vital for protecting our environment and community in Avadi, Chennai.

Waste Classification

We simplify the process by helping you sort electronic waste into three clear categories. This ensures each item is handled correctly to maximize its value and minimize environmental harm:
Reusable, Refurbishable, and Disposable.

Why It Matters

Improperly handled e-waste contains hazardous materials that can pollute our air, water, and soil. By recycling through our center, you help conserve valuable natural resources, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and prevent environmental contamination, creating a safer future for everyone.
